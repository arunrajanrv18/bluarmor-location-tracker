Battery Drain

Percentage drop per hour — lower is better

Battery drain (%/h)
How much battery the location tracker consumes per hour at each update interval
OnePlus A6000 at 5s is a critical outlier at 16.76 %/h — drained in ~6 hours. Moto g67 is most efficient (0.46–1.88 %/h). Intervals ≥ 15s keep all devices under 4.5 %/h.

Mean Temperature

Average device temperature during each test session (°C)

Mean temperature (°C)
Higher update frequencies drive up CPU/GPS usage and device temperature
All devices stay in a safe thermal range. The 15s interval is notably the coolest (25–29°C mean). At 1s, mean temps reach 34–37°C — sustained operation could trigger throttling on weaker SoCs.
